## Initial Justification for Investment

Based on increased global unrest and geopolitical tensions, I expect demand for Palantir's data analytics and intelligence software to grow significantly. Palantir's platforms are used by government agencies and commercial clients for critical data analysis, and I believe they are well-positioned to benefit from increased spending on security and intelligence solutions. Also Palantir has been increasing its focus on using AI and machine learning capabilities, which could drive future growth and profitability. I expect Palantir to continue expanding its customer base and increasing revenue as it capitalizes on these trends.

I invested in Palantir during the dip in its stock price, that occurred early in the US Iran conflict. The stock price decline presented an attractive entry point for me to invest in a company that I believe has strong growth potential in the current global environment.
